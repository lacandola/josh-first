Where To Go: 2-Day Travel in Tokyo, Japan!

Looking for a place to travel abroad this upcoming holiday season? I've got you covered!

In this article we'll feature:

  • Two places to visit in Tokyo
  • The best restaurants in the area
  • Some activities you can try during your stay

Day One: Akihabara, Japan

Akihabara is known for its Otaku Culture and wide variety of anime goods and electronics

  1. Best Restaurants

    2. Be sure to try the following restaurants:

    • Marugo (丸五) - this restaurant serves some of the area’s best tonkatsu
    • Motenashi Kuroki (饗くろ喜) - their Shio Ramen is a must-try
    • Namaiki (生粋) -offers premium quality meats and specializes in yukhoe (steak tartare)

  2. Activities You Can Do

    3. Here are some fun things to do here:

    • The Classic Maid Cafes
    • Buy Oden from a Vending Machine
    • Go to Mandarake Akihabara, mecca for manga and anime fans!


Day Two: Shibuya, Japan

Shibuya is known for its Japanese Fashion and Youth Culture

  1. Best Restaurants

    2. Be sure to try the following restaurants:

    • Hakushu Teppanyaki - Prepare for an interactive and sizzling Wagyu experience
    • Kipposhi - their Bright Blue Ramen is one-of-a-kind
    • Gonpachi - this restaurant blends traditional design with a vibrant atmosphere

  2. Activities You Can Do

    3. Here are some fun things to do here:

    • Brave the Shibuya Crossing
    • Explore and Go Shopping at the Shibuya Scramble Square
    • Spend some peaceful and refreshing time at the Miyashita Park
